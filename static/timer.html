Pomodoro Timer - Free Online Focus Timer

Use this free Pomodoro Timer to boost your productivity and study sessions. Features include customizable timer intervals, lo-fi background music for focus, screen wake lock to keep your display on, and an alarm system with increasing urgency. All preferences are saved locally in your browser - no account needed. Works on desktop, tablet, and mobile.

Features

  • Customizable timer: 1, 5, 10, 15, 20, 25, 30, 45, 60, 90, or 120 minutes
  • Auto-format: mm:ss for short timers, hh:mm:ss for longer ones
  • Alarm with increasing beep frequency, auto-reset after 30 seconds
  • Screen wake lock to prevent your device from sleeping
  • Background lo-fi music from YouTube for studying
  • Keyboard shortcuts: S to dismiss alarm, Space to start/pause
  • Session tracking with local storage
  • Beautiful aesthetic dark theme
